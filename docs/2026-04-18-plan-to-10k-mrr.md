**Date:** 2026-04-18
**Status:** strategic reset after Claude Design shock, grounded in codebase audit + enriched contacts + team transcripts
**Gate:** €10k MRR triggers full-time + fundraise + hiring conversations. Nothing before.

## The thesis in one paragraph

Basquio is not an AI deck generator. It is the **AI-native workspace for consumer insights analysts in FMCG**, running on the customer's own data (NIQ, Kantar, Circana, retailer 1P, internal), with 192KB of encoded FMCG domain knowledge as a structural moat. The deck is one output among several; the real product is context memory plus cross-source intelligence that no single-source data incumbent (NIQ, Circana, Kantar) can replicate because they are commercial enemies of each other. The 12-18 month window against NIQ Ask Arthur and Circana Liquid AI is the clock.

## Two commercial motions (Veronica Apr 18 session) + one organic PLG tail

The Apr 18 session with Veronica defined **two B2B motions**. Rossella's Apr 17 individual-user point is a distribution tactic inside the PLG tail, not a third primary motion. Clear separation:

### Motion 1 — Agency-output (Crosby shape, sell the work)

Fixed-price deliverable retainer. Procurement buys it as agency line item, not SaaS subscription. This is Crosby.ai applied to FMCG insights.

- **Buyer:** CMO, Head of Marketing, Head of Insights with autonomous budget authority
- **Pricing:** €2-5k/mo fixed for 3-5 guaranteed deliverables (decks, memos, workbooks), 48-hour turnaround
- **Payment:** corporate procurement as agency retainer, not SaaS
- **Pitch:** "AI-native insights firm for mid-market FMCG, delivery speed and price traditional consultancies cannot match"
- **Target:** Italian mid-market CPG where a CMO/Head signs with own budget (Caffè Borbone / Loacker / Bauli / GranTerre / Gruppo Montenegro / Campari mid-tier, PE-backed portfolios, boutique consulting firms using Basquio as delivery engine)
- **Structural shape:** Crosby exactly — fixed-price deliverable with AI engine + human-in-the-loop
- **Key constraint:** works when buyer has budget autonomy. Does not work for top-100 global CPG where everything escalates to corporate Legal.

### Motion 2 — Workspace SaaS (Legora shape, sell the tool + workflow)

The real Veronica insight. Per-seat SaaS with **context engineering as the moat**: memory per client, connectors to data sources (NIQ / Kantar / Circana / retailer 1P / internal ERP), ingestion of emails / meetings / brief documents / prior decks, team collaboration, CRM primitives. The analyst's daily working surface. Deck generation is one output among several.

- **Buyer:** Head of Insights / Head of Analytics / CMI leader at mid-market CPG insights team
- **Pricing:** €3-10k/seat/yr, **10-seat minimum → €30-100k floor ACV**, annual contract
- **Payment:** corporate procurement as SaaS line item (matches existing SaaS budget shape)
- **Pitch:** "AI-native workspace for consumer insights teams. Runs on your NIQ + Kantar + retailer + internal data. Team memory compounds every week."
- **Target:** mid-market CPG with 5-25 person insights function (Barilla-scale, Lavazza-scale, Ferrero-scale), plus ambitious SMB teams, plus PE-backed portfolios scaling their insights capacity
- **Structural shape:** Legora exactly — vertical AI workspace with per-seat pricing, memory compounding, forward-deployed ex-practitioner support
- **Key differentiation:** cross-source (NIQ Ask Arthur and Circana Liquid AI structurally cannot do this, single-source). The 192KB encoded domain knowledge + 10 FMCG validators make the workspace category-aware from day one.
- **Current state:** not yet shipped as a product surface. Web app multi-tenant foundation is there (organizations, memberships, per-workspace auth). Discord bot has the ingestion + search + CRM primitives. Gap is the web-native analyst UI + external connectors + context memory wiring per organization_id.

### Organic PLG tail (existing, secondary)

Already running. Not a motion to "push" strategically but revenue-positive and doesn't conflict with Motions 1 and 2.

- **Buyer:** individual analyst, freelance consultant, marketing manager expensing on personal card
- **Pricing:** Starter €19/mo, Pro €149/mo (already shipped and live)
- **Traffic:** launch video on LinkedIn + TikTok, warm intros from Ale + Francesco + Rossella network, organic SEO from Basquio Research content, directory listings (Uneed, G2, Capterra, PH)
- **Rossella's NIQ distribution tactic fits here:** Stefania and Silvia in "Chris Messina" role (2-month trial cap, verbatim Rossella Apr 17 voice), product validation + internal word-of-mouth to NIQ colleagues who then subscribe personally. Avoids corporate legal wall entirely.

### Later tiers (post €10k MRR proof)

- **Enterprise tier** for top-100 CPG with custom pricing and dedicated forward-deployed support
- **RMN channel** for retailer media networks (Walmart Scintilla, Kroger 84.51°, dunnhumby, Roundel, Carrefour Links) as white-label supplier insights backend

## Competitive picture (audited, sourced)

### Not real threats at €10k MRR

- **Claude Design** (Anthropic, research preview Apr 17 2026): horizontal consumer 20-slide decks. No FMCG domain. No cross-source. Kills Gamma, Tome, Beautiful.ai and every horizontal AI deck wrapper. Does not touch vertical FMCG-domain-encoded Basquio.
- **Gamma** ($100M ARR, $2.1B): horizontal wrapper, multiple compression imminent. Different category.
- **Auxi.ai**: PowerPoint add-in formatter for Big 4 consulting. Different distribution rail, sells formatting not analysis.
- **Microsoft Copilot for PowerPoint**: bundled with M365, weak at tabular data to narrative.

### Real competitors, 12-18 month window

- **NIQ Ask Arthur** (beta March 2026): NIQ's AI layer, single-source NIQ only, corporate slow. Owns the data Basquio runs on.
- **Circana Liquid AI + Brainwave**: same shape, single-source Circana only.

### Structural moat

NIQ and Circana are commercial enemies. Kantar and Circana same. No single-source vendor can aggregate competitor data into their own AI. Basquio is not a data provider: Basquio runs on whatever data the customer already licenses. Customer brings NIQ + Kantar + retailer 1P + internal, Basquio unifies. Structurally impossible for $4B+ single-source incumbents to replicate without acquiring each other.

## Kill conditions

Thesis dies if any of these hit. Re-check monthly.

- **NIQ Ask Arthur or Circana Liquid AI ships production-grade white-label** before end of Q1 2027 with the ability to produce 60-100 slide template-faithful decks for non-NIQ/non-Circana customers (i.e. they somehow solve cross-source).
- **Motion A conversion < 0.5% after 3 months** of outreach and video-driven traffic. Means the pitch or pricing is wrong for individual users.
- **Motion B accepts zero pilots after 3 months** of outbound to CMO-level Italian mid-market. Means agency-output shape is wrong for this segment.
- **Veronica doesn't actually use Basquio on real Victorinox work.** Signal that ICP fit is weaker than claimed.
- **Team velocity drops below 50 commits/week** while Marco remains part-time.

If 2+ of these hit at the same monthly review: freeze plan, regroup, reconsider shape.
